Seth Crail

Composer and Web Designer

Seth Crail is a passionate musician who loves finding new ways to be involved in music production. With two degrees from the Frost School of Music at the University of Miami, Seth is currently residing in Los Angeles as a composer, orchestrator, multi-instrumentalist and educator. He has worked with a long list of highly acclaimed educators and musicians including Carlos Rivera, Maria Schneider, Gary Lindsay and John Daversa.
